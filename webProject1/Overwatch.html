Overwatch League

    About Overwatch League:
  • Overwatch League is a professional league made by Blizzard Games. They made the game of Overwatch and the Overwatch League. Teams are created by big businesses and other organizations. For the first season of Overwatch League, there were 12 teams. These teams are anywhere from 8 to 12 players. The season playoff and grand finals were amazing! For season 2 of the Overwatch League, they added another 4 teams to make a total of 16 teams in the league.
